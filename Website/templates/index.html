What is SANI?

SANI stands for Semantic Assistance with Neural Intelligence

SANI is a conversatioanl A.I , type of advanced form of generative chatbot to which users can openly talk about any topics.

SANI can easily engage with people on a level that feels personal and even empathetic – like talking to a helpful assistant
